About

Engineer, technician, and coordinator built for remote environments

I operate at the intersection of technology, engineering, and human impact. With a foundation in industrial engineering and hands-on IT systems work, I lead operations where standard infrastructure does not exist.

From restoring a Terex Magnum 140 asphalt plant in Hassi Khebi, Béchar, to coordinating 5+ national medical caravans across Algeria's southern border regions, my focus is adaptability and real-world execution. I manage multidisciplinary teams, restore critical systems, and solve operational challenges in real time.

Working style: action-oriented, solution-driven, leadership under pressure. Fluent in Arabic, French, and English for cross-cultural coordination.

02

Impact

Coordinating healthcare for 10,000+ beneficiaries

Problem: Underserved communities in southern and border Algeria lacked consistent access to medical care, requiring large-scale mobile intervention.

Action: As Technical National Coordinator, I led logistics, field operations, and multidisciplinary medical teams across 5+ missions. Coordinated with local authorities and health institutions, managed supply chains, and resolved real-time challenges in remote desert conditions.

Result: Delivered direct healthcare access to more than 10,000 people, establishing repeatable operational models for future national caravans.

03

Engineering Case Study

Restoring critical infrastructure under field conditions

Location: Hassi Khebi, Béchar — Terex Magnum 140 Asphalt Plant

Problem: Complete plant shutdown threatening a critical infrastructure project. Limited technical support available on site.

Action: Conducted full system diagnosis. Repaired and restored conveyor system, burner, and filtration units. Recovered and recalibrated the SYSTEX control system without factory support.

Result: Returned plant to full operational capacity, eliminating downtime and enabling project continuation in a remote environment.
